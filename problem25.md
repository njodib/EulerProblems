nth term in fibonacci sequence is first to contain 1000 digits.

$
F^n > 10^{999} \\
\implies \log_{10}(F_n) > 999 \\
\implies \log_{10}\left(\frac{\phi^n}{\sqrt{n}}\right) > 999 \\
\implies n\log_{10}(\phi)-\log_{10}(\sqrt{5}) > 999 \\
\implies n > \frac{999-log_{10}(\sqrt{5})}{log_{10}(\phi)} \\
\implies n > 4781.85 \\
\implies n \ge 4782
$
